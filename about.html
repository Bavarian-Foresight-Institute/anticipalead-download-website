About the project

AnticipaLead

AnticipaLead is a scenario-based serious game designed to immerse teams in the challenges of leading AI transformations. It replaces the traditional classroom setting with a self-guided, highly interactive experience that helps players understand both the strategic and ethical implications of integrating artificial intelligence into various organizational sectors. The game is free for educational purposes and provides all necessary materials for immediate play.

About the project partners

armasuisse/deftech

Deftech (Defense Technology) is an initiative by armasuisse, the Swiss Federal Office for Defense Procurement. It anticipates technological developments and analyzes their potential impact on the military and society. By exploring disruptive innovations and fostering dialogue, armasuisse/deftech ensures that future challenges are addressed proactively and strategically.

Bavarian Foresight Institute

The Bavarian Foresight Institute, based at THI Ingolstadt, is dedicated to scientific research and applied future studies. It develops methodologies to anticipate societal and technological shifts, equipping leaders with the tools needed to make robust, future-proof decisions. Their expertise strongly guided the pedagogical foundation of AnticipaLead.
