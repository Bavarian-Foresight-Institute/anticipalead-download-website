How it works

Ready in four steps

Everything you need is in the download package. Follow the steps below to set up your first session.

01
Choose your scenario
Pick from 8 scenarios spanning AI in HR, healthcare, manufacturing, logistics, and more. Each challenges a different leadership dilemma.
02
Download and print
The assistant bundles exactly the files you need. Technology cards print at A4; canvases at A3.
03
Set up the table
Arrange materials as shown in the quick-start guide. The audio file replaces a human moderator entirely.
04
Play and reflect
Work through Canvas 1, draw technology cards, and discuss on Canvas 2. Repeat rounds, then share results across groups.
